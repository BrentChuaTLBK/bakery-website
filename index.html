About Us

Welcome to The Little Baker Kitchen! Founded by Chef Brent, we specialize in crafting delicious pastries and custom cakes for your special occasions.

From humble beginnings to representing the Philippines at an international pastry competition, we’re dedicated to making your celebrations unforgettable. Join us in sharing happiness, one treat at a time!
