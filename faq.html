Product

For Icing cakes: Icing cakes should be refrigerated in their original box to maintain freshness, especially if they contain perishable ingredients like fresh fruit or cream. Once the cake is sliced, it needs to be stored in an airtight container.

For fondant cakes: Fondant cakes should be stored in an air-conditioned room in their original box. Once the cake is sliced, it needs to be refrigerated and stored in an airtight container.

For pastries and other items: Pastries and other products should be stored in an airtight container. Depending on the type, they can be kept at room temperature or refrigerated. Please refer to specific product instructions for optimal storage conditions.

Most cakes and pastries are best enjoyed within 2-3 days of purchase. For specific storage instructions and shelf life, please refer to the guide instructions provided with your order or contact us for details.
